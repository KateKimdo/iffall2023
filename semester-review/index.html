Recipe Page

I designed a recipe page in creating a Dalgona candy, which became famous after being on the show, Squid Game. This is a personal favorite candy that contains so many memories of when I was young, buying this candy everyday after school. This was my first time creating a website out of scratch, which was both an interesting and great learning experience. I am very happy with how it turned out with just basic information but with responsive elements that can be viewed on my phone screen.

Microsite - Designer, Developer

With the direction from my Creative director, Laura Weil, I was able to create a website with the topic of Newtro. I have never knew what Newtro was until I was given with this topic even though I am Korean, which was a great experience of getting to know my culture even more. It was quite a struggle creating navigation bar and different html pages for the first time, but I am proud of how it came through and I had so much fun with it.
